How to buy our products

Purchasing our products is a straight forward process! Please follow the easy below.

1) Create an account with us and complete your profile.

2) Select a product from our inventory.

3) Add the product to your cart and wait for confirmation from us

4) We will contact to let you know if your order is approved, or if there are any problems by email or call from our staff

5) Transer the cost of the product to our bank account in accordance to the invoice we have issued

6) After the payment is confirmed, we will contact you once again by email or call.

7) The product will arrive to the shipping address you provided in 1-2 weeks

If you require any assistance in regards with the above procedures, please contact us.
